💡 Hint

Try placing crops in different plots. Avoid planting the same type repeatedly in the same place.

💡 Notes on Rotation

  • Heavy feeders (corn, pumpkin) should not follow themselves; they should come after legumes or root crops.
  • Legumes (beans) can come after heavy feeders or light feeders.
  • Light feeders (tomato, lettuce) can follow legumes.
  • Root crops (carrot, potato) improve soil structure and often go after light feeders or legumes.
